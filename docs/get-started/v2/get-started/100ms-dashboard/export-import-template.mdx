Export templates directly from the dashboard and import them into any workspace or account.

## How-to guide

<StepsContainer id="import-export-template">

### Export Template

![Export Template](/docs/v2/100ms-dashboard/export-template.png)

1. Click on **Templates** in the sidebar, and pick a template you want to export 
1. Click on the three dot menu near the top right
1. Click on **Export Template**, and you're done!

You should now see exported template.json in your downloads.

### Import Template

<video loop="true" autoplay="autoPlay" controls="false" id="vid" muted>
    <source src="/docs/docs/v2/100ms-dashboard/import-template.mp4" type="video/mp4" />
</video>

<Note type='white' title="Note: Select correct workspace and account">
    Make sure that you are logged in to the correct account and you've selected the correct workspace before importing your template
</Note>

<br></br>

1. Click on **Create Template**
1. Click on **Import Template** and choose the template.json you exported in the previous step
1. Enter a name, subdomain, and region for your template and click on **Exprience**

You're done. Your template should now be available in your current workspace and account.

</StepsContainer>
